Gameplay! Dark souls has a very unique combat system in the game. you can be killed in one hit about 90% of the time. That means defense is the best offense.

Difficulty. It is true Dark Souls 3 is a tough game. It is not however, the hardest game ever. Many people forget to make that distinction.
