Zelda is always a great game. The story of this game is great. The game does a great job of putting together a dark story with an upbeat franchise.

Sidequests are amazing in this game. Each sidequest is important and helps out your character. Most include new items and armors that help the players get an advantage agains the enemies.

Nostalgia. This was the first big main story quest game that I played as a child and it I always love it.
